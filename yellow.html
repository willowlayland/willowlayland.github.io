Ched Shreds

2021

A concept for better, more sustainable cheese packaging.

Most shredded cheese packaging comes in plastic baggies, but Ched Shreds are packaged in a reusable, plastic-less package. In addition to being made of sustainable materials, the Ched Shreds packaging allows for shredded cheese dispensation without having to directly use your hands. Most baggie packaging requires a person scoop out the cheese for sprinkling- CHED knows that after a pandemic, that sort of practice will die. So enjoy pouring out some shreds!

Women are Change Makers

2020

Brand redesign.

WOMEN ARE CHANGEMAKERS is a Nevada charity that hosts fundraising events for their sister charity, Zawadisha, which provides small loans to rural Kenyan women to help finance their livelihoods.

I wanted to design a logo that uses the graphic from Zawadisha and also connects Nevada to the cause. The face of Nevada is on the northern side, alluding to the focus of the charity to northern Nevada, and the face of Africa is on the eastern side for Kenya as well. I did not pursue a logo that connects to Kenya specifically because the Zawadisha logo chose to use the entire continent; so I followed that lead. The rebrand is more local, and human.
