LaGuardia Airport is an airport in East Elmhurst, Queens, New York City. Covering 680 acres (280 ha), the airport is the third busiest airport serving New York City, and the twentieth busiest in the United States.

LaGuardia Airport

2021

This redesign centers on an icon that evokes L and G lettermarks while also symbolizing the turning of a new page for the airport. The page motif is replicated throughout the airport as a paper plane, a cute mark. There are strong directionals throughout.

Additionally, it was important to keep the interior identity simple and timeless so that the airport does not age and become outdated once again. This is an identity made to last another hundred years.
