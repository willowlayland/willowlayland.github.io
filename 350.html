Conceptual BBQ

2022

A disposable coffee cup. It is presented with a spill of brown teeth instead of coffee beans. I ask the question, is having a daily caffeine intake a habit, routine, or addiction? A few things are sacrificed when a person buys coffee every day: the cups aren’t recyclable, their teeth and general health are impacted. But these impacts aren’t obvious. We can toss the cup out of sight, we will never see our own mouths outside of mirrors and pictures. A daily cup of joe is a habit that becomes an addiction because the consequences are not tangible, but are made so with this model.
