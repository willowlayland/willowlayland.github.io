Ear Soar

2019

Being on a commercial plane has many eye sores-- the solution is Ear Soar!

Ear Soar is an audio-visual game for airplane trips. Pleasant sounds through noise-reducing headphones trains the minds to better solve cognitive connect-the dots games. Plus, there are levels to learn some simple braille words. The app also allows passengers to keep track of their flight's progress, so they know how much time they have left to enjoy it. With Ear Soar the time passes by faster and the passenger can experience a little of hearing-impaired and blind ways of life.

The Lyricysts

2021

The Lyricysts are a fictional American folk rock band based in Denver, Colorado. The band’s stripped back raw sound would be similar to artists such as Bruce Springsteen, Bob Dylan and Tom Petty.

Folk, as a genre, draws from stories and the inherent nature of humanity. Songs don’t always serve as a call to action, rather it is meant to foster connection by going back to the songwriting and storytelling core of music.
